## How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

Akomi collects personal information such as:

- Name  
- Job title  
- Employer name  
- Work address  
- Work email  
- Work phone number  

We use this information to provide our services and do not sell personal information. We only share it with third parties who help facilitate our services.

We may also collect information from third-party websites (e.g., LinkedIn) or partners to enrich your profile data.

## Transferring Personal Data to the U.S.

Akomi processes personal data in the United States.

While the U.S. is not deemed “adequate” under GDPR Article 45, we use standard contractual clauses and other safeguards for data transfers, including:

- Consent-based processing  
- Contractual necessity  
- Legitimate interest  

We have received **zero government data requests** since founding. Contact us at [privacy@akomi.com](mailto:privacy@akomi.com) for more information.

## Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR and similar laws, you have the right to:

- Be informed  
- Access your data  
- Correct your data  
- Erase your data  
- Restrict processing  
- Data portability  
- Object to processing  
- Avoid automated decision-making  

To exercise these rights, contact [privacy@akomi.com](mailto:privacy@akomi.com). Reasonable access is provided free of charge unless otherwise justified.

## Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored on U.S.-based cloud servers.

- **Customer data**: Retained for the duration of the relationship and a period thereafter for legal, operational, and archival purposes.  
- **Prospect data**: Retained until no longer of business value.  
- **Deletion requests**: Data can be deleted upon verified request from a data subject or authorized agent.  

Contact [privacy@akomi.com](mailto:privacy@akomi.com) for more details.
