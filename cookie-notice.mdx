This Cookie Notice explains how Akomi, Inc. ("Akomi", "we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website or use our services.

1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small data files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use
We use the following types of cookies on our website:

a. Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cookies are essential for the operation of our website and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually set in response to actions made by you, such as logging in, filling out forms, or setting privacy preferences.

Example: Authentication tokens, session management

b. Performance and Analytics Cookies
These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our website. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and anonymous.

Example: Google Analytics, PostHog

c. Functional Cookies
These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers.

Example: Language preferences, region settings

d. Targeting or Advertising Cookies
We do not use targeting or advertising cookies on the Akomi platform at this time.

3. Third-Party Cookies
Some cookies on our website are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. These providers may use cookies to collect information about your interactions with our services. We review these providers regularly to ensure alignment with our privacy standards.

4. How You Can Control Cookies
You can manage or disable cookies directly through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact your experience on our website.

For Chrome: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647

For Safari: https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201265

For Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences

Alternatively, you may adjust your cookie preferences at any time by visiting our Cookie Settings (placeholder for UI-based consent manager, if you plan to implement one).
