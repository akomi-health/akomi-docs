## Medicare WISeR

Akomi supports prior authorization for Medicare fee-for-service through the CMS [Wasteful and Inappropriate Service Reduction (WISeR)](https://www.cms.gov/priorities/innovation/innovation-models/wiser) model. WISeR applies to select services in six pilot states: Texas, Oklahoma, New Jersey, Ohio, Arizona, and Washington.
